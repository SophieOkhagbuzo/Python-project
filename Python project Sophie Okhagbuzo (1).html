What is the structure of your dataset?

This loan dataset contains information about various loans issued by ProsperLoan. The structure of ths dataset includes the following info/types of columns:Loan information, borrower information,Loan status , repayment information.

What is/are the main feature(s) of interest in your dataset?

The main features of interest in my loan dataset are as follows;

  1. Loan_Status: This is the primary feature of interest, especially when assessing loan performance. We need to know whether loans are approved, rejected, paid off, or defaulted.
  2. Loan_Amount: The principal amount borrowed is crucial for understanding the scale of the loan, its risk, and its impact on the lender's portfolio.
  3. Interest_Rate: This determines the cost of borrowing for the borrower and the potential revenue for the lender. It is key in assessing profitability and risk.
  4. Borrower Credit Score

Credit_Score: A critical predictor of loan repayment behavior. Higher credit scores generally indicate lower risk of default. 5. Loan Term Loan_Term: The duration of the loan affects the monthly payment amount and the overall interest paid over time. Its important for understanding repayment schedules and loan affordability. 6. Repayment Information Monthly_Payment: This indicates the borrower's monthly financial obligation and can be used to assess affordability and the likelihood of default. Remaining_Balance: Helps in tracking the loans lifecycle and understanding how much of the loan is still outstanding. 7. Borrower Income Borrower_Income: Used to assess the borrowers ability to repay the loan. Higher income typically suggests a lower risk of default.

What features in the dataset do you think will help support your investigation into your feature(s) of interest?

The three most important features in my loan dataset, particularly for assessing risk and making lending decisions, are:

Loan Status: Indicates the current state of the loan (approved, defaulted, paid off). Its crucial for evaluating loan performance and understanding repayment behavior.

Credit Score: A key predictor of a borrowers likelihood to repay the loan. It directly impacts risk assessment and the decision to approve or deny a loan.

Loan Amount: The principal borrowed amount, which determines the potential risk and financial exposure for the lender.
